then the area of the rectangle is increased by 12
if instead
the width is increased by 2 then the area of the rectangle is increased by 42

+2 width = +42 area -> 2 w : 42 a

(l+1) . w = area + 12
lw + 1w = lw + 12
1w = 12
w = 12

area + 42 = (w+2) l - since area is just l . w we can substitute
lw + 42 = (w+2) l
lw + 42 = lw + 2l
42 = 2l
l = 21

threfore thw eidth = 12 and length = 21
and the area = 12 . 21 = 252
