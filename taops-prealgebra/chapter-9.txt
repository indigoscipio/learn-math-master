2. x = sqrt(36) -> x^2 = 6 which is can only be 6

9.1.4
how can we simplify sqrt(n^2) if n is negative?

answer:
if n is negative then n^2 is positive since it has even exponentso its the same as sqrt(n^2)
so then sqrt(n^2) is just positive n, in other words its the absolute value of n = |n| or
-n

eg n = -4
sqrt(-4^2) = sqrt(-1^2.4^2) = sqrt(1.4^2)=sqrt(4^2)=4
