Problem 14.3
Given two integers a and b, with b> a, find a formula for how many integers
there are between a and b inclusive. (Remember, inclusive means that we include a and b in our count.)

answer:
b - a + 1

example:
a = 4, b = 19
19 - 4 = 15
plus the one to make sure we count the end
15 + 1 = 16

alternative solution
a, a+1, a+2, ..., b
-(a-1), -(a-2), ..., -(a-1)
1,2,3,...,b-a+1
